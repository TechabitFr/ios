Nugget Tutorial (for dynamic island and etc)

Requirements: iOS 16.0-18.1

PLEASE READ INSTRUCTIONS CAREFULLY!

On your iPhone, download this shortcut by tapping here. It will ask you where to save the file so save it on your iPhone or iCloud, just know where it is at.

If you have a Mac, you can press share on the file you saved from the shortcut and AirDrop it.. For Windows, use Gmail and mail the file from your iPhone to your PC/Windows Computer.

Now you need to download an app on your Windows or Mac, choose the one that is your platform and open it: Mac (Apple Silicon) | Windows

When applying changes, select the mobile gestalt
